# T5.2 · Visual Data Analysis

**Location:** `textbook/5/analytics.tex:240–243`
**Textbook context:** Collaborative video analysis, object detection, privacy and inference.
**Data Foundry features:** Media Dataset, Annotation Dataset
**Platform pages:** [Media Dataset](https://data-foundry.net/Learning/Datasets/DatasetsOverview/mediadataset.html) · [Annotation Dataset](https://data-foundry.net/Learning/Datasets/DatasetsOverview/annotationdataset.html)

## Steps

1. **Video Card Game setup** – Export the thematic clips prepared in T4.2. Run a 30-minute collaborative session (2–4 people): watch, sort, discuss. Record the group's agreed-upon themes and contested interpretations.
2. **Object detection (optional)** – Apply a pre-trained YOLO or COCO model (via Python) to a sample of frames. Compare what the algorithm detects with what your video log noted.
3. **Ground truth comparison** – Manually annotate 20 frames in the Annotation Dataset. Compute precision/recall of the automated detection against your manual labels.
4. **Privacy audit** – Review what the object detector infers beyond what participants consented to share. Could any detection reveal sensitive information? Document your assessment.
5. **Reflect** – What did collaborative analysis reveal that solo analysis did not? What does the algorithm see that humans tend to overlook, and vice versa?
